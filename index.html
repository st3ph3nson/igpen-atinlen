EN Pig Latin Translator

Rules: Consonant-start words move the starting consonant cluster to the end + “en” (the → eth-en, car → arc-en). Vowel-start words just add “en” (are → areen). “I” and “is” now also take “en” (I → Ien, is → isen). Punctuation and spacing are preserved. Decode uses a best-guess heuristic (Pig Latin is inherently ambiguous).
